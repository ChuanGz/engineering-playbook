# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when they improve the practical value of this playbook.

## Expectations

- Open a proposal before substantial changes.
- Explain context, evidence, trade-offs, and intended outcome.
- Keep discussion respectful, factual, and focused on the work.
- Follow the writing and content quality standards.

This repository is not a public opinion board. Low-effort, promotional, copied, mass-generated, or unsupported personal-opinion content may be closed without further review. Maintainers retain final editorial and merge authority.
